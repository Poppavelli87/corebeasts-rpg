# Code of Conduct

## Our Commitment

We want this project to be welcoming, respectful, and safe for everyone.

## Expected Behavior

- Be respectful and constructive.
- Assume good intent.
- Give clear technical feedback.
- Focus on ideas and code, not people.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, threats, or discriminatory language.
- Personal attacks or hostile behavior.
- Sharing private information without permission.

## Reporting

If you experience or witness unacceptable behavior, report it privately to the maintainers.
Maintainers will review reports and respond in good faith.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may remove comments, reject contributions, or ban users for repeated or severe violations.
